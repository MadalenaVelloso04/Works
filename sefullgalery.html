Project for university 3rd semester 2023

Project Overview

Sustainablearth is an environmental consultancy committed to assessing and managing the environmental impact of human activities, with a particular focus on supporting small and medium-sized businesses in Africa. The organization's mission revolves around promoting responsible development through ecosystem restoration, risk management, and environmental education. With values deeply rooted in sustainability, Sustainablearth empowers individuals and businesses to embrace sustainable practices through pragmatic, actionable solutions and an inclusive, transparent approach.

Concept and Creation
of the Brand

The brand identity of Sustainablearth emphasizes a clean and natural aesthetic, inspired by elements like rivers and leaves to represent the harmony between human activity and the environment. The color palette, grounded in earthy tones and greens, evokes a sense of calm and connection to nature. Using simple and elegant typography, the brand communicates with a positive and empowering tone, positioning itself as a trusted, informative resource. The logo, combining the leaf and river imagery, reflects the consultancy's commitment to environmental stewardship and sustainable innovation.

Concept and Creation of
the Applications

Sustainablearth's branding extends into practical applications, including business cards, posters, stationery, and other branded materials. Each of these items carries the same simplicity and elegance seen in the core brand identity, ensuring that every interaction with the brand reinforces its values and vision. The posters, for example, are designed to be visually impactful yet uncluttered, aiming to capture attention and communicate the brand's commitment to a greener future.

Working on Sustainablearth was such a great experience for our team. It was our first time creating a full visual identity for an actual client, and doing it in university made it even more exciting. We got to take what we'd learned in class and apply it to a real-world project that's all about positive environmental impact. It was a rewarding way to dive into the world of brand design and collaboration!
